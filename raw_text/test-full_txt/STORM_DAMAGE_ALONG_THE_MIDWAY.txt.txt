<s> <s> September 23 , 1893 </s>
<s> <s> STORM DAMAGE ALONG THE MIDWAY . </s>
<s> <s> Reckoning After the Tempest Shows Many Dilapidated Tents and Signs . </s>
<s> <s> The Storm of Thursday night was the most severe of the season , according to the automatic instruments at the Weather Bureau exhibit . </s>
<s> <s> Rain commenced falling at 8:10 p.m. , and between 8:14 and 8:26 one-fifth of an inch fell , being at the rate of 2.50 inches an hour , while from 8:15 to 8:18 it fell at the rate of six inches an hour . </s>
<s> <s> At 8:10 the wind blew at the rate of sixty-six miles an hour , while the temperature fell 10 Â° . </s>
<s> <s> Considerable damage was done on Midway . </s>
<s> <s> At the Indian Village the main tent was blown down and it was not until noon yesterday that it was replaced . </s>
<s> <s> The Ferris Wheel kept people in who did not want to go out in the storm and closed the ticket offices , taking those in around twelve times . </s>
<s> <s> Two engineers who happened to be in the wheel said they would not take a large sum of money for the experience . </s>
<s> <s> At the wheel the weather gauge registered a velocity of sixty-six miles an hour for the wind . </s>
<s> <s> The Lapland Village had a great deal of canvas blown away . </s>
<s> <s> Forty big stag heads adorned the tent , each with full branching horns , and these fell among the girl performers as the tent went down , but no one was badly bruised . </s>
<s> <s> The Laplanders in all the tents were drowned out and had to take refuge in the offices . </s>
<s> <s> In Old Vienna some windows were blown in and awnings ruined , but the chief loss was caused by the people who went away in the excitement without paying their bills , it being estimated that the loss was $ 600 . </s>
