<s> <s> Ralston Purina Co . reported a 47% decline in fourth - quarter earnings , reflecting restructuring costs as well as a more difficult pet food market . </s>
<s> <s> The St . Louis company earned $45 . 2 million , or 65 cents a share , compared with $84 . 9 million , or $1 . 24 a share , a year earlier . Sales in the latest period were $1 . 76 billion , a 13% increase from last year's $1 . 55 billion . </s>
<s> <s> For the year ended Sept . 30 , Ralston earned $422 . 5 million , or $6 . 44 a share , up 8 . 9% from $387 . 8 million , or $5 . 63 a share . </s>
<s> <s> This year's results included a gain of $70 . 2 million on the disposal of seafood operations . </s>
<s> <s> Sales for the full year were $6 . 6 billion , up 13% from $5 . 8 billion . </s>
<s> <s> Ralston said its restructuring costs include the phase - out of a battery facility in Greenville , N . C . , the recent closing of a Hostess cake bakery in Cincinnati and a reduction in staff throughout the company . </s>
<s> <s> The battery plant , which makes rechargeable nickel cadmium and carbon zinc products , will be closed over the next year or so , a spokesman said . </s>
<s> <s> Ralston attributed its fourth - quarter slump partly to higher costs of ingredients in the pet food business as well as competitive pressures , which required higher advertising spending . </s>
<s> <s> For the year , pet food volume was flat , the company said . </s>
<s> <s> Its cereal division realized higher operating profit on volume increases , but also spent more on promotion . </s>
<s> <s> The Continental Baking business benefited from higher margins on bread and on increased cake sales , it added . </s>
<s> <s> Ralston said its Eveready battery unit was hurt by continuing economic problems in South America . </s>
<s> <s> Ralston shares closed yesterday at $80 . 50 , off $1 , in New York Stock Exchange composite trading . </s>
