<s> <s> POMPEII TO-DAY </s>
<s> <s> Behind the roofless porches of the forum are other ruined buildings — where the officers of the city did business , where the citizens met to vote , where tailors spread out their cloth and sold robes and cloaks . </s>
<s> <s> One large market building is particularly interesting . </s>
<s> <s> You will enter a courtyard with walls all around it and signs of lost porches . </s>
<s> <s> Broken partitions show where little stalls used to open upon the court . </s>
<s> <s> Other stalls opened upon the street . </s>
<s> <s> In some of these the excavators found , buried in the ashes and charred by the fire , figs , chestnuts , plums , grapes , glass dishes of fruit , loaves of bread , and little cakes . </s>
<s> <s> Were customers buying the night ’s dessert when Vesuvius frightened them away ? </s>
<s> <s> In a cool corner of the building is a fish market with sloping marble counter . </s>
<s> <s> Near it in the middle of the courtyard are the bases of columns arranged in a circle around a deep basin in the floor . </s>
<s> <s> In the bottom of this basin the excavators found a thick layer of fish scales . </s>
<s> <s> Evidently the masters used to buy their fish from the market in the corner . </s>
<s> <s> Then the slaves carried them here to the shaded pool of water and cleaned them and scaled them and washed them . </s>
<s> <s> In another corner the excavators found skeletons of sheep . </s>
<s> <s> Here was a pen for live animals which a man might buy for his banquet or for a sacrifice to his gods . </s>
<s> <s> His slave would lead the sheep away through the crowds . </s>
<s> <s> But on that terrible day when the volcano belched , the poor bleating animals were deserted . </s>
<s> <s> Their pen held them and the ashes covered them and to-day we can see their skeletons . </s>
<s> <s> The walls around the market are still standing , though the top is broken and the roof is fallen . </s>
<s> <s> They are still covered with paintings . </s>
<s> <s> If you will look at them you can guess what used to be for sale here . </s>
<s> <s> There are game birds and fish and wine jars all pictured here in beautiful colors . </s>
<s> <s> There are cupids playing about a flour mill and cupids weaving garlands . </s>
<s> <s> There are also pictures of the gods and heroes and the deeds they did . </s>
<s> <s> Imagine this painted market full of chattering people , the little shops gay with piles of beautiful fruit and vegetables , the graceful columns and dark porches adding beauty . </s>
<s> <s> Imagine these people crying out and running and these columns swaying and falling when Vesuvius bellowed and shook the earth . </s>
<s> <s> And yet we can see the very fruits that men were buying and the pictures they were enjoying . </s>
<s> <s> The forum with its markets and shops and offices and temples and statues was the very heart of the city . </s>
<s> <s> Many streets led into it . </s>
<s> <s> Perhaps you will walk down one of them , between broken walls , past open doorways . </s>
<s> <s> After several street corners you will come to a large building with high walls still standing and with tall , arched entrance . </s>
<s> <s> This also was one of the gay places in Pompeii , for it was a bathhouse . </s>
<s> <s> Every day all the ladies and gentlemen of the town came strolling toward it down the streets . </s>
<s> <s> The men went in at the wide doorway . </s>
<s> <s> The women turned and entered their own apartments around the corner . </s>
<s> <s> And as they walked toward the entrance they passed little shops built into the walls of the bathhouse . </s>
<s> <s> At every stall stood the shopkeeper , bowing , smiling , begging , calling . </s>
<s> <s> “ Perfumes , sweet lady ! ” </s>
<s> <s> “ Rings , rings , beautiful madam , for your beautiful fingers ! ” </s>
<s> <s> “ Oil for your body , sir , after the bath ! ” </s>
<s> <s> “ A taste of sweets , madam , before you enter ! </s>
<s> <s> Honey cakes of my own making ! ” </s>
<s> <s> “ Do n’t forget to buy my dressing for your hair before you go in ! </s>
<s> <s> You ’ll get nothing like it in there . ” </s>
<s> <s> So they chattered and called and coaxed . </s>
<s> <s> Some of the people bought , and some went laughing by and entered the bathhouse . </s>
<s> <s> As the gentlemen went in , a large court opened before them . </s>
<s> <s> Here were men bowling or jumping or running or punching the bag or playing ball or taking some other kind of exercise before the bath . </s>
<s> <s> Others were resting in the shade of the porches . </s>
<s> <s> A poet sat in a cool corner reading his verses to a few listeners . </s>
<s> <s> Some men , after their games , were scraping their sweating bodies with the strigil . </s>
<s> <s> Others were splashing in the marble swimming tank . </s>
<s> <s> Here and there barbers were working over handsome gentlemen — smoothing their faces , perfuming their hair , polishing their nails . </s>
<s> <s> There was talk and laughter everywhere . </s>
<s> <s> Men were lazily coming and going through a door that led into the baths . </s>
<s> <s> There were large rooms with high ceilings and painted walls . </s>
<s> <s> In one we can still see the round marble basin . </s>
<s> <s> The walls are painted with trees and birds and swimming fish and statues . </s>
<s> <s> It was like bathing in a beautiful garden to bathe here . </s>
<s> <s> Another room was for the hot bath , with double walls and hot air circulating between to make the whole room warm . </s>
<s> <s> The bathhouse was a great building full of comforts . </s>
<s> <s> No wonder that all the idle Pompeians came here to bathe , to play , to visit , to tell and hear the news . </s>
<s> <s> It was a gay and noisy place . </s>
<s> <s> We have a letter that one of those old Romans wrote to a friend . </s>
<s> <s> He says : </s>
<s> <s> “ I am living near a bath . </s>
<s> <s> Sounds are heard on all sides . </s>
<s> <s> The men of strong muscle exercise and swing the heavy lead weights . </s>
<s> <s> I hear their groans as they strain , and the whistling of their breath . </s>
<s> <s> I hear the massagist slapping a lazy fellow who is being rubbed with ointment . </s>
<s> <s> A ball player begins to play and counts his throws . </s>
<s> <s> Perhaps there is a sudden quarrel , or a thief is caught , or some one is singing in the bath . </s>
<s> <s> And the bathers plunge into the swimming tank with loud splashes . </s>
<s> <s> Above all the din you hear the calls of the hair puller and the sellers of cakes and sweetmeats and sausages . ” </s>
<s> <s> After you leave the baths perhaps you will turn down Stabian Street . </s>
<s> <s> It has narrow sidewalks . </s>
<s> <s> The broken walls of houses fence it in closely on both sides and cast black shadows across it . </s>
<s> <s> It is paved with clean blocks of lava . </s>
<s> <s> You will see wheel ruts worn deep in the hard stone . </s>
<s> <s> Almost two thousand years old they are , made by the carts of the farmers , perhaps , who brought in vegetables for the market . </s>
<s> <s> At the street crossings you will see three or four big stone blocks standing up above the pavement . </s>
<s> <s> They are stepping-stones for rainy weather . </s>
<s> <s> Evidently floods used to pour down these sloping streets . </s>
<s> <s> You can imagine little Roman boys skipping across from block to block and trying to keep their sandals dry . </s>
