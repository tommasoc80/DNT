<s> <s> China 's state leadership transition has taken place this month against an ominous backdrop . </s>
<s> <s> More than 16,000 dead pigs have been found floating in rivers that provide drinking water to Shanghai . </s>
<s> <s> A haze akin to volcanic fumes cloaked the capital , causing convulsive coughing and obscuring the portrait of Mao Zedong on the gate to the Forbidden City . </s>
<s> <s> So severe are China 's environmental woes , especially the noxious air , that top government officials have been forced to openly acknowledge them . </s>
<s> <s> Fu Ying , the spokeswoman for the National People 's Congress , said she checked for smog every morning after opening her curtains and kept at home face masks for her daughter and herself . </s>
<s> <s> Li Keqiang , the new prime minister , said the air pollution had made him `` quite upset '' and vowed to '' show even greater resolve and make more vigorous efforts '' to clean it up . </s>
<s> <s> Last month , after deadly air pollution hit record levels in northern China , officials led byWen Jiabao , then the prime minister , put forward strict new fuel standards that the oil companies had blocked for years . </s>
<s> <s> But there are doubts about whether the oil companies will comply , especially since oil officials resisted a similar government order for higher-grade fuel four years ago . </s>
<s> <s> State-owned power companies have been similarly resistant . </s>
<s> <s> The companies regularly ignore government orders to upgrade coal-burning electricity plants , according to ministry data . </s>
<s> <s> And as with the oil companies , the power companies exert an outsize influence over environmental policy debates . </s>
<s> <s> In 2011 , during a round of discussions over stricter emissions standards , the China Electricity Council , which represents the companies , pushed back hard against the proposals , saying that the costs of upgrading the plants would be too high . </s>
<s> <s> On Feb. 28 , Deutsche Bank released an analysts ' note saying that China 's current economic policies would result in an enormous surge in coal consumption and automobile sales over the next decade . </s>
<s> <s> `` China 's air pollution will become a lot worse from the already unbearable level , '' the analysts said , calling for drastic policy changes and `` a strong government will to overcome the opposition from interest groups . '' </s>
<s> <s> The report estimated that the number of passenger cars in China was on track to hit 400 million by 2030 , up from 90 million now . </s>
