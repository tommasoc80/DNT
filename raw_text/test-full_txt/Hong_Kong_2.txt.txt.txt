<s> <s> Hong Kong </s>
<s> <s> What to Buy Antiques . </s>
<s> <s> Hollywood Road in the Mid-Levels above Central is the most famous antiques street in Hong Kong . </s>
<s> <s> Look for fine Chinese bronzes , embroidery , lacquerware and porcelain , tomb figures , and wood carvings , among other possibilities . </s>
<s> <s> The experts point out that it is not age alone that determines a Chinese antique ’s value — the dynasties of the past had their creative ups and downs . </s>
<s> <s> For serious antiques , try Honeychurch Antiques at no. 29 for furniture and silver , Tai Sing Company at 122 for porcelain . </s>
<s> <s> For fun you can visit the Low Price Shop at no. 47 or the Cat Street crafts stores and flea market . </s>
<s> <s> Brocades and Silks . </s>
<s> <s> Fabrics from China are a bargain and well worth taking home . </s>
<s> <s> Chinese-product department stores stock silk fabrics , silk scarves , finely embroidered blouses , and traditional padded jackets . </s>
<s> <s> Chinese Arts and Crafts is at Pacific Place in Central , and in Star House in Tsim Sha Tsui ; CRC Department Store is on Hennessy Road in Causeway Bay . </s>
<s> <s> For fabrics , also try Western Market , Morrison Street , in Central . </s>
<s> <s> Cameras . </s>
<s> <s> Photo buffs know that Hong Kong is the place to buy some of the world ’s most advanced photographic equipment , and there are some real bargains around . </s>
<s> <s> However , be sure you compare prices and models before buying . </s>
<s> <s> Two reliable places to start looking in Lan Kwai Fong are Photo Scientific in the Eurasia Building and Hing Lee Camera Company , 25 Lyndhurst Terrace . </s>
<s> <s> Carpets and Rugs . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong is a mecca for Chinese hand-knotted wool carpets and silk rugs . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong ’s stores are usually able to arrange shipment . </s>
<s> <s> Caravan at 65 Hollywood Road and the shops in The Silk Road at Ocean Center in Tsim Sha Tsui are good places to start looking . </s>
<s> <s> China ( Porcelain ) . </s>
<s> <s> In Hong Kong you can have a plate , or even a whole dinner service , hand-painted to your own design . </s>
<s> <s> Factories in Kowloon and the New Territories , producing traditional and modern china , are geared to entertain and instruct visiting tourists ; prices are appealing . </s>
<s> <s> Two of the largest places to go are the Wah Tung China Company in the Grand Marine Industrial Building in Aberdeen ; and the Overjoy Porcelain Factory in Block B of the Kwai Hing Industrial Building , Kwai Chung , in the New Territories . </s>
<s> <s> In antiques shops , look for highly valued porcelains from China . </s>
<s> <s> Note that because of the duty-free situation , good bargains may be found in European china , including Spode and Wedgwood . </s>
<s> <s> Electronics . </s>
<s> <s> The latest gadgets are sometimes available in Hong Kong before anywhere else . </s>
<s> <s> Before you begin shopping , pick up HKTA ’s “ Shopping Guide to Consumer Electronics . ” </s>
<s> <s> Prices on electronics have risen in the past two years ; check prices at home before you buy here . </s>
<s> <s> Nathan Road has many electronics shops . </s>
<s> <s> Also check out Star Computer City in the Star House near the Star Ferry terminal . </s>
<s> <s> Furniture . </s>
<s> <s> The choice ranges from traditional hand-carved Chinese rosewood furniture to well-made reproductions of modern Western styles . </s>
<s> <s> Rattan furniture is highly popular . </s>
<s> <s> Hollywood Road has several furniture shops . </s>
<s> <s> Queen ’s Road East in Wan Chai is a furniture manufacturing and retail area . </s>
<s> <s> Jade . </s>
<s> <s> “ Good for the health ” is just one of the many magical qualities that are attributed to these beautiful emerald-green or turquoise stones . </s>
<s> <s> Real jade is extremely expensive , and you may be offered counterfeit jade , which looks exactly like the genuine article . </s>
<s> <s> Some people say you can test the authenticity by touch — real jade feels smooth and cool . </s>
<s> <s> Alternatively , you can shine a lamp on the stone — real jade shows no reflected light . </s>
<s> <s> Better still , go shopping with an expert . </s>
<s> <s> Jewelry . </s>
<s> <s> Thanks to the duty-free situation , prices in Hong Kong are lower than they are in some other places . </s>
<s> <s> You can buy gemstones loose or set , or have them made up to your own design . </s>
<s> <s> Popular purchases include diamonds and freshwater pearls . </s>
<s> <s> If you do plan to buy jewelry , be sure to consult the “ Shopping Guide to Jewellery ” published by the Hong Kong Tourist Authority to find a reputable dealer . </s>
<s> <s> Kitchen Equipment . </s>
<s> <s> Woks and any other gadgets essential for Chinese cookery make good purchases . </s>
<s> <s> Department stores sell all sorts of intriguing kitchen equipment . </s>
<s> <s> Leather Goods . </s>
<s> <s> Leather is not a great bargain in Hong Kong . </s>
<s> <s> Locally made items do not live up to their European models . </s>
<s> <s> However , the leather garment industry is growing , and there is a wide range of locally produced leather accessories , all at extremely attractive prices . </s>
<s> <s> For European imports , you will pay top dollar . </s>
<s> <s> Musical , Audio , and Video Equipment . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong has a vast range of the most high-tech audio-visual , sound , and screen equipment . </s>
<s> <s> Before purchasing , visitors should make sure of compatibility with systems in their own countries . </s>
<s> <s> Be sure to look around and compare before buying . </s>
<s> <s> Whatever you buy , you may be able to work out a discount . </s>
<s> <s> Ready-to-wear Clothes . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong ’s shops carry almost every recognizable European and many American labels , from top-end designers to the moderately priced or trendy . </s>
<s> <s> Nathan Road , Central , and the hotel malls are places to look . </s>
<s> <s> There are still a great many factory outlet stores with reasonable prices . </s>
<s> <s> You ’ll also find bargain clothes for sale at the markets and on push-carts . </s>
<s> <s> Tailoring . </s>
<s> <s> Tailor-made clothes are not as popular in Hong Kong as they were in the past , but hundreds of shops still remain . </s>
<s> <s> Local tailors are experts when it comes to producing custom-tailored garments for both men and women , and are also adept at copying patterns . </s>
<s> <s> The result can be a quality suit at a fair price — but made-to-measure clothing is not cheap . </s>
<s> <s> In choosing a tailor , look for HKTA membership . </s>
<s> <s> Many tailors have Web sites or are listed on Web sites . </s>
<s> <s> Tea . </s>
<s> <s> Shops all over town will sell you gift tins of exotic blends . </s>
<s> <s> If you want to learn something about tea , go to the Tea Shop at 149 Hollywood Road , or the Moon Garden Tea House at 5 Hoi Ping Road , Causeway Bay . </s>
<s> <s> The owners will brew up a pot so you can taste before making a choice . </s>
