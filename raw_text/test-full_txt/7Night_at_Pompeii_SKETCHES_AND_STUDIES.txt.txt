<s> <s> Night at Pompeii </s>
<s> <s> On to Pompeii in the clear sunset , falling very lightly upon mountains , islands , little ports , and indentations of the bay . </s>
<s> <s> From the railway station we walked above half a mile to the Albergo del Sole under a lucid heaven of aqua-marine colour , with Venus large in it upon the border line between the tints of green and blue . </s>
<s> <s> The Albergo del Sole is worth commemorating . </s>
<s> <s> We stepped , without the intervention of courtyard or entrance hall , straight from the little inn garden into an open , vaulted room . </s>
<s> <s> This was divided into two compartments by a stout column supporting round arches . </s>
<s> <s> Wooden gates furnished a kind of fence between the atrium and what an old Pompeian would have styled the triclinium . </s>
<s> <s> For in the further part a table was laid for supper and lighted with suspended lamps . </s>
<s> <s> And here a party of artists and students drank and talked and smoked . </s>
<s> <s> A great live peacock , half asleep and winking his eyes , sat perched upon a heavy wardrobe watching them . </s>
<s> <s> The outer chamber , where we waited in armchairs of ample girth , had its loggia windows and doors open to the air . </s>
<s> <s> There were singing-birds in cages ; and plants of rosemary , iris , and arundo sprang carelessly from holes in the floor . </s>
<s> <s> A huge vase filled to overflowing with oranges and lemons , the very symbol of generous prodigality , stood in the midst , and several dogs were lounging round . </s>
<s> <s> The outer twilight , blending with the dim sheen of the lamps , softened this pretty scene to picturesqueness . </s>
<s> <s> Altogether it was a strange and unexpected place . </s>
<s> <s> Much experienced as the nineteenth-century nomad may be in inns , he will rarely receive a more powerful and refreshing impression , entering one at evenfall , than here . </s>
<s> <s> There was no room for us in the inn . </s>
<s> <s> We were sent , attended by a boy with a lantern , through fields of dew-drenched barley and folded poppies , to a farmhouse overshadowed by four spreading pines . </s>
<s> <s> Exceedingly soft and grey , with rose-tinted weft of steam upon its summit , stood Vesuvius above us in the twilight . </s>
<s> <s> Something in the recent impression of the dimly lighted supper-room , and in the idyllic simplicity of this lantern-litten journey through the barley , suggested , by one of those inexplicable stirrings of association which affect tired senses , a dim , dreamy thought of Palestine and Bible stories . </s>
<s> <s> The feeling of the cenacolo blent here with feelings of Ruth 's cornfields , and the white square houses with their flat roofs enforced the illusion . </s>
<s> <s> Here we slept in the middle of a contadino colony . </s>
<s> <s> Some of the folk had made way for us ; and by the wheezing , coughing , and snoring of several sorts and ages in the chamber next me , I imagine they must have endured considerable crowding . </s>
<s> <s> My bed was large enough to have contained a family . </s>
<s> <s> Over its bead there was a little shrine , hollowed in the thickness of the wall , with several sacred emblems and a shallow vase of holy water . </s>
<s> <s> On dressers at each end of the room stood glass shrines , occupied by finely dressed Madonna dolls and pots of artificial flowers . </s>
<s> <s> Above the doors S. Michael and S. Francis , roughly embossed in low relief and boldly painted , gave dignity and grandeur to the walls . </s>
<s> <s> These showed some sense for art in the first builders of the house . </s>
<s> <s> But the taste of the inhabitants could not be praised . </s>
<s> <s> There were countless gaudy prints of saints , and exactly five pictures of the Bambino , very big , and sprawling in a field alone . </s>
<s> <s> A crucifix , some old bottles , a gun , old clothes suspended from pegs , pieces of peasant pottery and china , completed the furniture of the apartment . </s>
<s> <s> But what a view it showed when Christian next morning opened the door ! </s>
<s> <s> From my bed I looked across the red-tiled terrace to the stone-pines with their velvet roofage and the blue-peaked hills of Stabiae . </s>
