<s> <s> February 24 , 1884 </s>
<s> <s> The Lynching of John Heith </s>
<s> <s> TOMBSTONE , Arizona , Feb. 23 . </s>
<s> <s> â€” </s>
<s> <s> At 9 o'clock on Thursday morning Judge Pinney sentenced John Heith to confinement in Yuma Penitentiary for life for complicity in the Bisbee murders . </s>
<s> <s> Twenty-four hours later the dead body of Heith dangled from the cross bar of a telegraph pole near the foot of Toughnut Street , where it was suspended by a rope . </s>
<s> <s> The following are the particulars of the occurrence as near as can be gathered : About 8:30 yesterday morning a crowd of men , mostly miners , numbering about 150 , proceeded to the Courthouse . </s>
<s> <s> Arriving there they detailed seven of their number from Bisbee , who entered and demanded that John Heith be turned over to them . </s>
<s> <s> The seven men approached the door leading to the corridor of the jail and one of them knocked . </s>
<s> <s> Being about time for the Chinaman who brings food for the prisoners to arrive , Jailer Ward opened the door unsuspiciously , and was immediately covered by weapons and told to give up the keys of the jail . </s>
<s> <s> Seeing any attempt at resistance would be useless he did as requested , and in a few minutes the deputation was in the presence of the sought-for man . </s>
<s> <s> The crowd , which by this time had filled the spacious hall , started for the street . </s>
<s> <s> At the door they were met by Sheriff Ward , who called on them in the name of the law to desist . </s>
<s> <s> The Sheriff was picked up and gently removed down the steps out of the way , while the crowd started down the street on a run . </s>
<s> <s> The rope had been placed around Heith 's body , and about 20 men had hold of it . </s>
<s> <s> It never became taut during the run , the prisoner keeping up with the crowd , and showing no signs of the white feather . </s>
<s> <s> Arriving at the place selected for the hanging one of the party climbed a telegraph pole and passed the rope over the cross-bar . </s>
<s> <s> Heith pulled a handkerchief from his pocket and , placing it on his knee , coolly and deliberately folded it , and , placing it over his eyes , asked someone in the crowd to tie it . </s>
<s> <s> This being done , he informed the crowd they were hanging an innocent man , and would find it out when the others ( meaning Dowd and his companions ) were hanged . </s>
<s> <s> He told them he had faced death too often to be afraid , and had but one request to make , namely , that they would not shoot into his body . </s>
<s> <s> He was told his last wish would be respected and he told them he was ready . </s>
<s> <s> Countless hands grasped the rope . </s>
<s> <s> A run was made , and in a twinkling the man was suspended to the pole . </s>
<s> <s> The news spread about town rapidly , and in a few minutes an immense crowd of men , women , and children congregated on the scene . </s>
<s> <s> The universal expression was , " Served him right . " </s>
<s> <s> That this opinion should be so prevalent is no doubt the result of the testimony at the trial , which was convincing to any mind of ordinary intelligence , that Heith was a guilty accessory to the Bisbee murders . </s>
<s> <s> The Coroner 's jury found as a verdict that Heith came to his death from " emphysema , which might have been caused by strangulation , self-inflicted or otherwise . " </s>
<s> <s> A placard was posted on the telegraph pole where Heith was found suspended and dead with the following inscription : " John Heith was hanged to this pole by citizens of Cochise County for participation in the Bisbee massacre as a proved accessory at 8:20 A.M. , Feb 22 , 1884 to advance Arizona . " </s>
