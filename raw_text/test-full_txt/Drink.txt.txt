<s> <s> Drink </s>
<s> <s> Naples is becoming increasingly popular with a younger generation of both Italians and foreigners . </s>
<s> <s> In spite of false and stereotypical reports of adverse conditions , they flood into the city and lend renewed vitality to its nightlife . </s>
<s> <s> The hippest scene is around the bars and cafes on Piazza Bellini , Piazza Santa Maria la Nova and Piazza San Domenico Maggiore , becoming busy after about 11PM . </s>
<s> <s> You should also try the area around Piazza dei Martiri , expecially Vico Belledonne a Chiaia , where you can find many crowded bars , a winebar and lots of young people , expecially at weekends . </s>
<s> <s> However , if you are looking for a American/English/Northern European drinking establishments you may be hard pressed to find what you are looking for as that culture is frowned upon in Naples . </s>
<s> <s> There are a variety of small drinking establishments but if you 're looking for a crowded beer hall , Irish pub , or an American college-style dive bar , you 'll have trouble finding one . </s>
<s> <s> If you 're in Naples and wondering what local beverages to try , the first answer is that Naples is as famous for its extra-strong , semi-sweet coffee as it is for its pizza , and you can try some at places like Gran Caffe Aragonese with a little ice cream floating on top or at Caffe Gambrinus , which also serves cake and cocktails . </s>
<s> <s> Amazingly , coffee is so thoroughly Neapolitan that it is commonly sold at local bars as well . </s>
<s> <s> For those who would like to try the local beer and wine , there are an abundance of options . </s>
<s> <s> Beer bars were once rare , beer being traditionally sold with and consumed in pizza parlors , but now they are more common . </s>
<s> <s> Wine bars are classic in Naples , which is not surprising since it is the capital of Campania , a major wine-producing region . </s>
<s> <s> There are many local varieties of wine you may wish to sample , but the Aglianico is peculiarly appropriate . </s>
<s> <s> Aglianico black grapes are grown throughout southern Italy , but Campania provides them with their ideal soil and growing climate . </s>
