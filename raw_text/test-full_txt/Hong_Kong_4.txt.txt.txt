<s> <s> Hong Kong </s>
<s> <s> Sports Participant Sports Beaches . </s>
<s> <s> In subtropical Hong Kong you can swim from April to early November . </s>
<s> <s> There are more than 40 beaches in Hong Kong that are free to the public . </s>
<s> <s> Most have lifeguards on duty from April to October , changing rooms , toilets , and snack stands . </s>
<s> <s> On Hong Kong Island , Repulse Bay is the most popular ; others are Shek O on the east coast and Stanley and Deep Water Bay on the south coast . </s>
<s> <s> They are very crowded , especially on summer weekends . </s>
<s> <s> On the outlying islands , Cheung Chau and Cheung Sha are on Lantau , and Hung Shing Ye and Lo So Shing on Lamma ; inquire about water pollution levels . </s>
<s> <s> Golf . </s>
<s> <s> The Hong Kong Golf Club ( Tel. 2812 7070 ) welcomes visitors to its three 18-hole courses at Fanling in the New Territories , or the 9-hole course at Deep Water Bay . </s>
<s> <s> The Discovery Bay Golf Club on Lantau island ( Tel. 2987 7273 ) has an 18-hole Robert Trent Jones Jr. course , open to visitors Monday , Tuesday , and Friday . </s>
<s> <s> Many Hong Kong residents and visitors take the express train to Guangzhou to play at the Guangzhou Luhu Golf and Country Club ( Tel. 2317 1933 in Hong Kong or 020-8350 7777 ) . </s>
<s> <s> The 72-par course was designed by Dave Thomas . </s>
<s> <s> Hiking . </s>
<s> <s> In the New Territories the famous MacLehose Trail stretches 97 km ( 60 miles ) from Sai Kung Peninsula to Tuen Mun . </s>
<s> <s> The Lantau Trail is a 69-km ( 43-mile ) circular trail on Lantau Island that begins and ends at Silvermine Bay . </s>
<s> <s> Both trails are divided into smaller segments of varying difficulty . </s>
<s> <s> Maps of hiking trails are available at the Government Publications Center , Low Block , Government Offices , 66 Queensway in Central . </s>
<s> <s> HKTA also has trail maps and sponsors the Guided Nature Walks , led by rangers , that include hikes in all the different regions of Hong Kong . </s>
<s> <s> Jogging . </s>
<s> <s> Victoria Park has a jogging track in Causeway Bay . </s>
<s> <s> Sailing . </s>
<s> <s> Because of the heavy harbor traffic , only sailors licensed by the Hong Kong authorities can run pleasure boats in local waters . </s>
<s> <s> Contact the Hong Kong Yacht Club at Tel. 2832 2817 for information . </s>
<s> <s> Taijiquan ( Tai Chi ) . </s>
<s> <s> HKTA offers lessons in these exercises that improve concentration and balance at Garden Plaza , Hong Kong Park , Admiralty ( Tel. 2058 1234 ) . </s>
<s> <s> Tennis . </s>
<s> <s> There are 13 public courts at Victoria Park Tennis Centre ( Tel. 2570 6168 ) , near Tin Hau Station . </s>
<s> <s> Spectator Sports Horseracing . </s>
<s> <s> All levels of society share a feverish interest in the Sport of Kings . </s>
<s> <s> The racing schedule is September to June , and Hong Kong maintains two tracks — the older Happy Valley course on Hong Kong Island and the striking Sha Tin track in the New Territories . </s>
<s> <s> The Hong Kong Tourist Association runs a “ Come Horseracing Tour , ” which includes entry to the Hong Kong Jockey Club visitors ’ box and members ’ enclosure , and a buffet-style meal . </s>
<s> <s> Cricket . </s>
<s> <s> The Hong Kong International Cricket Series , held in late September , brings teams from all over the world . </s>
<s> <s> Rugby . </s>
<s> <s> The Rugby Sevens sees teams come together from all over the world for 15 matches in March or early April . </s>
<s> <s> Children ’s Hong Kong Hong Kong has many attractions that appeal to children of all ages . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong ’s many beaches are especially fun for children . </s>
<s> <s> Children love riding on Hong Kong ’s antique trams . </s>
<s> <s> A ride on the Peak Tram is sure to provide a thrill , and in the Peak Tower they ’ll enjoy the Peak Explorer ride and Ripley ’s Believe it or Not ! </s>
<s> <s> Ocean Park ( see page 32 ) is popular with children of all ages . </s>
<s> <s> There ’s a special Kid ’s World that those under 12 can enter free when accompanied by a paying adult . </s>
<s> <s> The more daring can try out the terrifying roller-coaster rides . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong ’s state-of-the-art interactive museums will interest children of all ages . </s>
<s> <s> The Science Museum in Tsim Sha Tsui East allows children to get their hands on over half of its 500 exhibits , while the nearby Space Museum has regular screenings on an enormous Omnimax screen in its Space Theater , making the night sky come vibrantly alive . </s>
<s> <s> For children who love boats , riding the Star Ferry or ferry trips to outlying islands will be exciting , and the Dolphin Watch trip ( see page 113 ) is certain to appeal . </s>
<s> <s> If you plan to visit during May , the carnival atmosphere of the Cheung Chau Bun Festival , with its high bamboo-and-paper towers covered in sticky buns , will fascinate the young ones . </s>
