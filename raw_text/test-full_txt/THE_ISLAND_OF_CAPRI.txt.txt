<s> <s> THE ISLAND OF CAPRI . </s>
<s> <s> The steamboat leaves Naples from St. Lucia between 8 and 9 a.m. Office , 34 Strada Molo Piccolo . </s>
<s> <s> Fare , 10 lire ; embarkation and landing , 80 centesimi each . </s>
<s> <s> Do not go if the wind is east or north , as you can not then enter the Blue Grotto . </s>
<s> <s> The island of Capri is 1,980 feet above the sea , and is noted for its scenery and as being the residence of Tiberius . </s>
<s> <s> " Caprese is an island disjoined from the point of the cape of Surrentum by a channel of three miles . </s>
<s> <s> I am strongly inclined to believe that Tiberius was taken with its perfect solitude . </s>
<s> <s> The temperature of the climate is mild in winter , from the shelter of a mountain , which intercepts the rigour of the winds . </s>
<s> <s> Its summers are refreshed by westerly winds , and are rendered delightful from the wide expanse of sea which the island commands " ( Tacitus , " Ann . " iv . 67 ) . " </s>
<s> <s> At a distance of eight miles from Surrentum is Capri , famous for the Villas of Tiberius . </s>
<s> <s> The island is eleven miles in circumference " ( Pliny , iii . 12 ) . </s>
<s> <s> THE BAY OF NAPLES . </s>
<s> <s> It is only from the deck of a vessel that one can have any idea of the beautiful situation enjoyed by Naples and its surrounding towns . </s>
<s> <s> The shore , the white buildings , the purple hills behind and the blue sky above ( perhaps tinted by the smoke from Vesuvius ) , and the deep blue sea , all go to make up an unequalled picture . </s>
<s> <s> THE BLUE GROTTO . </s>
<s> <s> ( Grotta Azzurra . ) </s>
<s> <s> Boat from landing - place for one to three people , 1 1/2 to 3 lire . </s>
<s> <s> Entrance to the Grotto , 1.25 each . </s>
<s> <s> Time , two hours . </s>
<s> <s> Passing the slight remains of the Baths of Tiberius , we arrive at the entrance of the grotto , which being only 3 feet high , visitors must recline in the boat . </s>
<s> <s> The height of the interior is 41 feet , with 8 fathoms of water . </s>
<s> <s> Length , 175 feet ; breadth , 100 feet . </s>
<s> <s> The blue reflection completely blinds the visitor at first ; and objects in the water have a silvery appearance . </s>
<s> <s> The boatman will plunge in , if you wish , for a lira ; but it is quite a treat to jump in oneself . </s>
<s> <s> The blueness is caused by the reflection of light . </s>
<s> <s> The morning steamer stops at the Blue Grotto , and then proceeds to the Grand Marina landing . </s>
<s> <s> Lunch at Restaurant de la Grotte Bleue . </s>
<s> <s> For visitors intending to make a stay , lunch should be had at Stanfords Hotel du Louvre , and bed ordered for the night . </s>
<s> <s> After lunch start off , with a boy as guide , for the VILLAS OF TIBERIUS . </s>
