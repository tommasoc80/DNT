<s> <s> March 6 , 1918 </s>
<s> <s> Laundry Car over Cliff </s>
<s> <s> Laundry strike sympathizers drove a Walker Laundry Company motor truck over Cliff Drive hill at Hardesty Avenue late this afternoon , after capturing the car and routing the driver and two special officers at Fourteenth Street and Euclid Avenue . </s>
<s> <s> One of the special officers fired a shot into the crowd before fleeing from the rain of bricks and stones . </s>
<s> <s> No one was injured . </s>
<s> <s> Homer Maze , 5106 East Twenty-fourth Street , was driving the laundry truck . </s>
<s> <s> Guarding him were two special officers , Sam Seaman , 2700 East Twenty-seventh Street , and C.L. Winner , 717 East Eleventh Street . </s>
<s> <s> Maze was making a delivery at Fourteenth street and Euclid Avenue when a crowd of about twenty-five man and women approached from the west and opened fire of rocks and stones on the standing car . </s>
<s> <s> Maze came from the house and made a run to join the special officers . </s>
<s> <s> After several minutes of fusillading stones , the officers and Maze deserted the car and reported the disturbance at the Flora Avenue Police Station . </s>
<s> <s> Seaman , one of the special officers , told of firing a shot toward the crowd , attempting to disperse the strike sympathizers . </s>
<s> <s> Re-enforcements joining the attacking party seemed to arrive steadily , they said , so they gave up the car to the crowd . </s>
<s> <s> When the police arrived at the scene of the disturbance a part of the crowd was yet there . </s>
<s> <s> Six men and one woman were arrested . </s>
<s> <s> The men could not be identified by Maze or the special officers as having thrown stones . </s>
<s> <s> The woman , Julia Anderson , 1711 We st Prospect Place , was identified by them and was held on a $ 51 cash bond . </s>
<s> <s> She denies having thrown anything . </s>
<s> <s> The truck was found after a search , but is practically demolished . </s>
<s> <s> A second `` wrecking party was reported from Eleventh Street and Chestnut Avenue . </s>
<s> <s> B. L. Ferguson , 6424 Lee Street , driver of a Kansas City Laundry Company truck , and a special officer , Salvator Schira , 1911 Missouri Avenue , were attacked by fifteen men and twelve women . </s>
<s> <s> A stone thrown by one of the striking laundry workers struck Ferguson on the cheek , another on the right hand . </s>
<s> <s> His injuries are not severe . </s>
