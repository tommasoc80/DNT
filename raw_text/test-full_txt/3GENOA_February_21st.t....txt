<s> <s> GENOA , February 21st . </s>
<s> <s> A note has just come from your cousin , Genevra Fuller , urging us to make her a visit at her home in San Remo . </s>
<s> <s> This invitation , which is most cordial , is a temptation to us all , but Zelphine and Angela have promised to spend their time with friends in Nice , stopping for a day at Monte Carlo , if their sporting tastes lead them so far afield , while I yield to Genevra 's blandishments . </s>
<s> <s> You know of old that she is not a person to be lightly refused when she has set her heart upon any given thing - a family trait , I believe . </s>
