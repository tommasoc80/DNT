<s> <s> Hong Kong </s>
<s> <s> What to Do </s>
<s> <s> Shopping </s>
<s> <s> Inflation has taken its toll in Hong Kong . </s>
<s> <s> While it ’s no longer the bargain shopping destination it once was , there are still some good buys to be had . </s>
<s> <s> Since Hong Kong is a duty-free port and charges no sales tax , goods are cheaper here than in the country where they were made . </s>
<s> <s> On photographic equipment , electronic goods , and watches , you avoid the luxury tax payable in your home country . </s>
<s> <s> Specialty goods and souvenirs , often handmade , come from Hong Kong and elsewhere in China . </s>
<s> <s> Custom-made garments by skillful Hong Kong tailors are still much in demand and cost less than elsewhere for comparable garments . </s>
<s> <s> Note that alcohol and tobacco are both exceptions to Hong Kong ’s duty-free regime and are subject to tax . </s>
<s> <s> You ’ll find that prices are about the same in Hong Kong Central and Kowloon , and somewhat cheaper in Causeway Bay , which caters to local shopping . </s>
<s> <s> Large shops on the fashionable thoroughfares tend to be more expensive than smaller “ family ” shops tucked away in the side streets . </s>
<s> <s> Stores do not open until 10am or later , but shopping goes on into the evening , up to 9:30 pm . </s>
<s> <s> Most shops are open seven days a week . </s>
<s> <s> Shops in Central are an exception ; they generally close at 6pm and are not open on Sunday . </s>
<s> <s> The only holiday on which all commerce comes to a halt is the Chinese New Year in January or February . </s>
<s> <s> Buyer Beware . </s>
<s> <s> Be aware that name brands , including electronics , are sometimes fakes , glass may be sold as jade , and that antique you bought may have been made last night . </s>
<s> <s> Always ask for a receipt that records information about the item , and if you buy an antique , be sure to get a certificate of authentication . </s>
<s> <s> Needless to say , avoid peddlers who approach you on the street and offer to take you to wondrous bargains . </s>
<s> <s> The large department stores have fixed prices , but elsewhere you should ask whether there is a discount , especially if you buy several items in one shop . </s>
<s> <s> Compare prices before you buy any significant item . </s>
<s> <s> Always ask to see the manufacturer ’s guarantee when purchasing watches , cameras , and audio-visual and electronic equipment . </s>
<s> <s> Note that when haggling , the merchant assumes you are prepared to pay cash . </s>
<s> <s> If , after concluding a deal , you try to pay with a credit card , he may then boost the price in order to cover the card charges . </s>
<s> <s> It is advisable to shop at outlets that are members of the Hong Kong Tourist Association ( HKTA ) , identified by a red junk logo . </s>
<s> <s> Membership imposes an obligation to maintain standards of both quality and service , and provides dissatisfied customers with an officially recognized channel for redressing complaints ; the number to call is Tel. 2508 1234 . </s>
<s> <s> Pick up a copy of HKTA ’s “ The Official Dining , Entertainment & Shopping Directory ” in which all member stores are listed . </s>
<s> <s> Shipping . </s>
<s> <s> Many stores will pack and ship purchases . </s>
<s> <s> Ask if automatic free insurance is provided . </s>
<s> <s> If the goods are very valuable or fragile , it is a good idea to buy an all-risk insurance for the shipment . </s>
<s> <s> Packages sent to the US or to Europe generally take six to eight weeks by surface mail , and one week by airmail . </s>
<s> <s> Shopping Areas . </s>
<s> <s> Major shopping areas are Tsim Sha Tsui in Kowloon , especially along Nathan Road ; Central on Hong Kong Island , particularly for upscale designer goods ; Causeway Bay for slightly better prices ; and the Hollywood Road area . </s>
<s> <s> Department Stores . </s>
<s> <s> Look for Lane Crawford Ltd. , an upscale store with branches at Pacific Place , 70 Queen ’s Road , and Harbour City ; Wing On , one of the oldest in Hong Kong ; Marks and Spencer ; and the Japanese department stores , Mitsukoshi , Sobo , and Seibu . </s>
<s> <s> Malls . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong is full of giant malls . </s>
<s> <s> Harbour City , just west of the Star Ferry Terminal in Tsim Sha Tsui is one of the largest ; Pacific Place , 88 Queensway , is Central ’s biggest mall , with retail outlets and department stores ; Times Square is a collection of retail outlets in Causeway Bay . </s>
<s> <s> In addition , most top-line hotels have upscale malls full of designer boutiques . </s>
<s> <s> Factory Outlets . </s>
<s> <s> These stores sell excess stock or factory overruns . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong is no longer a factory outlet center since much of its clothing manufacturing has moved elsewhere . </s>
<s> <s> There are factory showrooms in the Pedder Building , 12 Pedder Street , in Central . </s>
<s> <s> Markets . </s>
<s> <s> Markets are the places to use your bargaining skills . </s>
<s> <s> Hong Kong ’s most famous and colorful market is the Temple Street Night Market near the Jordan MTR stop . </s>
<s> <s> Every conceivable kind of goods is sold here : clothing , all kinds of electronics , CDs , souvenirs , crafts , and jewelry . </s>
<s> <s> Stanley Market is located on Hong Kong ’s southern coast , and is well-known for all kinds of clothing , including silk and cashmere . </s>
<s> <s> Bargain , and carefully examine any merchandise you buy here . </s>
<s> <s> The Jade Market , on Kansu Street in Yau Ma Tei , is known for both jade and freshwater pearls . </s>
<s> <s> This is not the place to make expensive purchases , but it ’s great for inexpensive pendants , earrings , and gifts . </s>
