<s> <s> Coleco Industries Inc . , a once high - flying toy maker whose stock peaked at $65 a share in the early 1980s , filed a Chapter 11 reorganization plan that provides just 1 . 125 cents a share for common stockholders . </s>
<s> <s> Under the plan , unsecured creditors , who are owed about $430 million , would receive about $92 million , or 21 cents for each dollar they are owed . </s>
<s> <s> In addition , they will receive stock in the reorganized company , which will be named Ranger Industries Inc . </s>
<s> <s> After these payments , about $225 , 000 will be available for the 20 million common shares outstanding . </s>
<s> <s> The Avon , Conn . , company's stock hit a high in 1983 after it unveiled its Adam home computer , but the product was plagued with glitches and the company's fortunes plunged . </s>
<s> <s> But Coleco bounced back with the introduction of the Cabbage Patch dolls , whose sales hit $600 million in 1985 . </s>
<s> <s> But as the craze died , Coleco failed to come up with another winner and filed for bankruptcy - law protection in July 1988 . </s>
<s> <s> The plan was filed jointly with unsecured creditors in federal bankruptcy court in New York and must be approved by the court . </s>
