<s> <s> May 30 , 1911 </s>
<s> <s> Four Cars in Thrilling Crash in Front of Main Grandstand . </s>
<s> <s> Wescott Mechanician Suffers Serious Hurts . </s>
<s> <s> S. P. DICKSON , OF AMPLEX NO. 44 , INSTANTLY KILLED </s>
<s> <s> CROWD IN GRAND STAND SHUDDERS AT CRASH NEAR PITS . </s>
<s> <s> FIRST REPORTED MANY DEAD </s>
<s> <s> Case Car Breaks Steering Gear — Hearne 's Fiat , Case ; Apperson Jack Rabbit and Wescott Clash — One Man , Crazed by Drive , Leaps From Car and Is Hurt </s>
<s> <s> INDIANAPOLIS MOTOR SPEEDWAY , May 30 . </s>
<s> <s> — </s>
<s> <s> One man was killed and half a dozen more or less seriously injured during the five-hundred-mile race this afternoon . </s>
<s> <s> Death came early in the race , but the sensational accident of the day was at 1:30 , when four cars were wrecked almost directly in front of the grand stand . </s>
<s> <s> The only person who was seriously hurt was John Glover , mechanician for Westcott car No. 7 , driven by Harry Knight who also was hurt . </s>
<s> <s> The other cars that were wrecked were Eddie Hearne 's Fiat No. 18 , Joe Jagersberger Case No. 8 and Lytle 's Apperson Jack Rabbit No. 35 . </s>
<s> <s> That several people were not killed was a mystery to the great crowd in the grand stands . </s>
<s> <s> The Case No. 8 , coming down the home stretch , carrying Jagersberger and his mechanician D. Anderson , broke a steering knuckle and crashed into the cement wall . </s>
<s> <s> The car rebounded to the center of the track with crushed wheels , and Anderson fell directly in the roadway . </s>
<s> <s> Could Only Check Racers . </s>
<s> <s> Starter Wagner made an effort to flag the racers that followed , but he could do little more than check their speed . </s>
<s> <s> Harry Knight in his Westcott , who was the closest to No. 8 , turned out toward the judge 's stand to avoid striking the prostrate mechanician , and his car skidded sideways at great speed . </s>
<s> <s> At the pits near the south end were Lytle 's car and Eddie Hearne 's Fiat No. 18 , which had stopped because of the tire trouble . </s>
<s> <s> Knight 's car crashed into them with such terrific force that both 18 and 35 were dragged along to the roadway opposite the gate . </s>
<s> <s> Cars Turn Over . </s>
<s> <s> The impact caused No. 7 to turn over , throwing Knight and his mechanician , John Glover , some distance . </s>
<s> <s> In the meantime Wagner was endangering his life in the center of the track checking the flyers . </s>
<s> <s> However , when it was seen that all the wrecked cars were free of the course he waved them ahead , and the men were tearing around like mad trying to make up for time lost . </s>
<s> <s> Taken to Hospital . </s>
<s> <s> At the hospital an examination showed that Knight was not as seriously injured as was first supposed and after being treated by some physicians he was sent to him home . </s>
<s> <s> Glover appeared to be the worse injured of the two and the physicians were unable to determine the exact nature of his injuries . </s>
<s> <s> When Anderson fell on the track he was not badly hurt and after lying there a few seconds he jumped up and limped to a place of safety . </s>
<s> <s> The accident happened in the most crowded part of the course , and perhaps half of the estimated attendance of 100,000 saw it . </s>
<s> <s> As if by common impulse the thousands of people in the stands arose and a cry of horror was heard . </s>
<s> <s> At first it was thought a number of people about the crowded pits had been killed . </s>
<s> <s> Death and serious injury was dealt , first in the thirtieth mile , and again when nearly 100 miles of the race had been run . </s>
<s> <s> The accident to the Amplex that resulted in the instant death of S. P. Dickson , mechanician , wrought up the crowd , and when Lozier car No. 34 , driven by Teddy Tetzlaff , was seen to veer in front of another car the spectators gasped . </s>
<s> <s> There was a crash , and the Lozier was overturned . </s>
<s> <s> Tetzlaff 's mechanician , David Lewis , suffered a broken leg , but Tetzlaff was only bruised . </s>
<s> <s> The car that crashed in from behind , Pope-Hartford , No. 5 , was withdrawn with the Lozier . </s>
<s> <s> The Pope occupants were not hurt . </s>
<s> <s> Had Narrow Escapes . </s>
<s> <s> Louis Disbrow and Richard Uliarechib , in Pope , had a narrow escpae . </s>
<s> <s> The cars were coming down the home stretch when at a point just north of the bridge Tetzlaff 's car burst a rear tire . </s>
<s> <s> This caused his machine to swerve into Disbrow 's car , overturning the Lozier and causing the Pope-Hartford to spin around several times on the track until it stopped near the pole . </s>
<s> <s> The Lozier also stopped near the pole and the men dragged the cars out of the way of the other racers that were thundering down the course . </s>
<s> <s> At the hospital where Lewis was taken an examination showed that his leg was broken . </s>
<s> <s> Tetzlaff was only shaken up . </s>
<s> <s> S. P. Dickon , mechanician for Arthur Greiner , driver of Amplex car No. 44 , was the first victim of the race . </s>
<s> <s> Shortly after the thirtieth mile , Greiner was sweeping around the back stretch when both rear tires exploded , wrecking the car . </s>
<s> <s> Dickson was instantly killed , Greiner was seriously injured . </s>
<s> <s> Greiner is a millionaire driver from Chicago , who came here and volunteered his services to the Amplex people , following the wreck of the Amplex car No. 12 several days ago , in which Joe Horan was injured . </s>
<s> <s> Taken to the Hospital . </s>
<s> <s> The ambulance was sent across the field and Greiner and the body of Dickson were taken to the hospital . </s>
<s> <s> After an examination it was said that Greiner had a fracture of the skull and a broken arm . </s>
<s> <s> The machine left the track and turned over , Dickson being crushed beneath it and Greiner being thrown a distance of about twenty-five feet . </s>
<s> <s> There had been more or less concern among the racing men for a week concerning the Amplex cars . </s>
<s> <s> One of them , No. 12 , had several accidents and was withdrawn . </s>
<s> <s> Dickson Lived in Chicago </s>
<s> <s> Dickson 's body was badly lacerated . </s>
<s> <s> After Greiner had been examined in the hospital the physicians said he did not seem to be as badly injured as was first supposed . </s>
<s> <s> Dickson lived in Chicago and his father is Major Dickson , on the editorial staff of the Chicago Record Herald . </s>
<s> <s> He was twenty-four years old and unmarried . </s>
<s> <s> He was known as an experienced man in the racing game . </s>
<s> <s> Many People Leave . </s>
<s> <s> However , all jumped to places of safety when they saw Knight 's car coming them broadside . </s>
<s> <s> Not a few of the horror-stricken people in the grand stand evidentally had enough of the race , which was only half through , and left the grounds . </s>
<s> <s> As it was there was a tremendous feeling of relief when it became generally known there were no lives lost in this accident . </s>
<s> <s> So much excitement was caused in the judges ' and timers ' stands that the time for the 250 miles was overlooked . </s>
<s> <s> Thrilling Escape From Injury . </s>
<s> <s> The crowd again was brought to its feet by the thrilling escape from injury or death of Howard Fry , driving a Mercer , as a substitute for Bigelow . </s>
<s> <s> Fry 's car skidded and plunged toward the repair pits across from the grandstand . </s>
<s> <s> He turned it back to the middle of the track , but the car was too wild for him to handle and swerved toward the pits again . </s>
<s> <s> Fry then kicked his brake and the car swung fully around before it stopped . </s>
<s> <s> Amid a storm of cheers from the crowd Fry resumed the race . </s>
<s> <s> Bob Evans Injured . </s>
<s> <s> Another accident that added one more to the list of injured , happened on the back stretch , about 2:30 . </s>
<s> <s> Bob Evans , a relief driver in charge of Jackson No. 26 , was sprinting along the stretch , when he attempted to turn out for another car . </s>
<s> <s> His racer skidded and when the wheels slipped from the edge of the track , Evans was thrown out and an ankle was broken . </s>
<s> <s> With the aid of his mechanician he climbed back into the car , which had come to a standstill , and drove back to the pits on the home stretch , from where he was sent to the hospital . </s>
<s> <s> Jack Tower , the regular driver , then took charge of the car and continued in the race . </s>
